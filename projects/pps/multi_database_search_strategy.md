## Database-Specific Notes

### PubMed/MEDLINE
- **Proximity operators** (`[tiab:~2]`): Finds terms within 2 words of each other
- **Supplementary Concepts**: Indexed terms not yet in MeSH tree (e.g., "psychogenic syncope", "somatic cough syndrome")
- **Expected results**: ~303 citations

### Cochrane CENTRAL
- **MeSH format**: `[mh "Term"]` instead of `"Term"[Mesh]`
- **Free-text fields**: `:ti,ab,kw` (title, abstract, keywords)
- **Proximity**: `NEAR/N` for terms within N words, `NEXT` for adjacent words
- **Expected results**: Likely lower than PubMed (trial-focused database)

### Embase
- **Controlled vocabulary**: Emtree (not MeSH) - **USER MUST VERIFY ALL TERMS**
- **Proximity**: `ADJ2` for terms within 2 words
- **Format**: Dialog syntax shown above; Ovid syntax available on request
- **Expected results**: Possibly higher than PubMed (broader biomedical coverage)

### CINAHL
- **Controlled vocabulary**: CINAHL Headings (similar to MeSH)
- **Interface**: EBSCO platform
- **Field codes**: `MH` (CINAHL Heading with +explosion), `TI` (Title), `AB` (Abstract)
- **Expected results**: Moderate (nursing and allied health focus)
- **Verify terms**: Use "Suggest Subject Terms" in EBSCO interface

### PsycInfo
- **Controlled vocabulary**: APA Thesaurus of Psychological Index Terms
- **Interface**: Ovid or EBSCO (Ovid syntax shown)
- **Field codes**: `exp` (explode thesaurus term), `.ti,ab.` (title/abstract)
- **Expected results**: Moderate (psychology/behavioral health focus)
- **Verify terms**: Use "Map Term to Subject Heading" in Ovid

### ClinicalTrials.gov
- **No controlled vocabulary**: All MeSH expanded to free-text synonyms
- **Structure**: Separate fields for Condition/Intervention/Other Terms
- **Limitations**: May miss relevant trials if condition/intervention not explicitly stated
- **Expected results**: Lower (trial registrations only)

### ICTRP (WHO)
- **Simple syntax**: Basic keyword matching across trial registries worldwide
- **No Boolean within fields**: Use separate searches or manual filtering
- **Coverage**: Includes non-US trials not in ClinicalTrials.gov
- **Expected results**: Variable (depends on registry coverage)
